Which molecular vector representation should we use?

David Huang 7/02/22
This blog post summarizes a quick investigation into the relative performances of difference molecular vector representations: descriptors, fingerprints, and learned representations. There is no universally clear answer for which molecular vector representation to use, and each has its own comparative advantages and disadvantages.
We'll be evaluating a molecular descriptor and fingerpting from Descriptastorus/RDKit--rdkit2dnormalized and morgan3counts--as well as an unsupervised learning technique--mol2vec--and our own semi-supervised contrastive learning technique--OlorenVec. The RDKit descriptors and Morgan fingerprints are industry standard representations and reflect longstanding chemoinformatics techniques.
